## WHAT TO GRAB NEXT (Recommendations)

Every 2 weeks Fab rotates new free assets. Always claim them all. Priority claims when they appear:

1. Any forest/nature environment pack (trees, foliage, landscapes)
2. Any medieval/viking building modular kit
3. Any animation pack (combat, locomotion)
4. Any crafting/survival system Blueprint
5. Any creature/enemy character with animations
6. Audio packs (ambient, SFX, music)
7. Any landscape/terrain material pack

Also claim the Niagara Examples Pack immediately — it's permanently free and has 50+ VFX you need.
